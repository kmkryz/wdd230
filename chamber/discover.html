Discover the Town of Trumbull

Discover Trumbull, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Connecticut. This charming town offers a unique blend of historical sites, picturesque parks, and vibrant community events that make it a perfect destination for both residents and visitors. From the serene trails of Twin Brooks Park to the educational treasures within the Trumbull Historical Society, there's something for everyone. Explore our bustling local businesses, from cozy cafes to innovative tech startups, and experience the warmth and inclusivity of our community gatherings. Whether you're looking to delve into the rich tapestry of our town's history, enjoy the natural beauty of our outdoor spaces, or support local commerce, Trumbull invites you to discover all the possibilities it holds. Join us and see why so many people are proud to call Trumbull home.

Upcoming Events

Annual Trumbull Art Fair

Date: Saturday, June 15th, 2024 | Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM | Location: Trumbull Town Hall Lawn

Join us for the Annual Trumbull Art Fair, showcasing the best local artisans and crafters. From paintings and photography to handmade jewelry and ceramics, explore a variety of art that celebrates our community's creativity. Enjoy live music, food stalls, and art workshops suitable for all ages. Admission is free, and all are welcome!

Trumbull Farmers Market

Date: Every Thursday from May to October 2024 | Time: 3:00 PM - 6:30 PM | Location: Unity Park

Experience the best of local agriculture at the Trumbull Farmers Market. Featuring fresh produce, organic goods, gourmet foods, and artisanal products, our farmers market is a feast for the senses. Support local farmers and businesses while enjoying a fun afternoon out with family and friends.

Trumbull Summer Concert Series

Date: Every Friday in July 2024 | Time: 7:00 PM | Location: Trumbull Town Green

Get ready for the Trumbull Summer Concert Series, a lineup of free outdoor concerts perfect for summer evenings. Bring a blanket or lawn chair and enjoy a variety of music genres under the stars. From jazz and blues to rock and pop, there's something for every music lover.

Cinema Under the Stars

Date: First Saturday of August 2024 | Time: Sundown | Location: Twin Brooks Park

Gather your family and friends for a magical evening at Cinema Under the Stars. Our annual outdoor movie night features a popular family-friendly film projected on a large screen in the beautiful setting of Twin Brooks Park. Popcorn and refreshments will be available for purchase. Don't forget to bring your blankets and lawn chairs!
